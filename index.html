Quickscreen makes eligibility screening for safety-net health services in Richmond faster and easier.

Learn more Get updates  

Most safety-net health providers require patients to go through a screening process that verifies their income, employment, household size, and insurance status. Itâ€™s often slow and confusing, and patients have to provide the same information repeatedly if they need care from more than one organization. Quickscreen lets health providers share a single patient eligibility profile, so they can spend less time collecting eligibility information and get patients into care faster.

"The safety net is somewhat connected, but having this system in place is a step in the right direction to move forward with a more coordinated approach to caring for the community together."

- Julie Bondy, Bon Secours Care-a-van

Quickscreen began as a collaboration between Code for America and the Richmond City Health District, with funding from Bon Secours, VCU, Richmond Memorial Health Foundation, the Community Foundation, and the Jenkins Foundation. Learn more about our partners here.

We are currently in an early pilot stage. Contact us to learn more, or sign up for updates below.
