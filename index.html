RVA Health

The RVA Health project connects safety-net clinics and patient eligibility information to expedite the screening process and get individuals to care faster.

Learn more  

The RVA Screening Project is a collaboration between Code for America and the Richmond City Health District. This website is the front page to the application, a host to the technical and user documentation, a space for us to collect interest, a place to host our blog permanently beyond the project, and a spot to host our other side projects.

You can read more about our partners here and take a look at the legal documentation in place that ensures all information we collect and share within the network is secure and used properly.

"We would like to think the safety-net is connected, this is a step in the direction of holding hands and moving forward together."

- Julie Bondy, Bon Secours Care-a-van

Much of this project has been driven by the health community in Richmond, VA. Our partners include:

  • Bon Secours
  • VCU
  • Daily Planet
  • Crossover
  • OAR
  • Richmond Center for High Blood Pressure
  • Richmond City Health District
  • Richmond Memorial Health Foundation

You can find everything you need to know about the screening application in our documentation section. This includes technical documentation and user documentation.
